Petrosains CRM Management System

Project Background

Client: Petrosains Sdn Bhd, a subsidiary of PETRONAS. The goal was to develop a unified CRM dashboard for top-level management with a consolidated view of income, expenditure, and predictive analysis.

Key Objectives

  • Centralize income and expenditure data in a single dashboard.
  • Integrate predictive analysis to forecast trends.
  • Visualize complex data in digestible chart-based formats.
  • Enable exporting reports in multiple formats.

User Research and Insights

Primary users were senior management and administrative teams.

  • Users preferred graphs and charts over text-heavy reporting.
  • Simplified data upload and export workflows were critical.
  • Custom date filters were high-priority across all data views.

Key Features and Functionality

  1. Dashboard: high-level metrics with quick links to revenue, expenditure, and predictive analysis.
  2. Revenue and Expenditure: CSV upload, interactive date filtering, and PDF export.
  3. Predictive Analysis: dynamic trend projections with configurable inputs.
  4. Reports: aggregated metrics and multi-format sharing options.
  5. Settings: target management and admin user creation controls.

Reflection and Impact

This project reinforced the value of stakeholder communication and iterative design for translating complex requirements into practical, impactful UI/UX solutions.
