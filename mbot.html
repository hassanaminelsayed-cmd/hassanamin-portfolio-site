Malaysia Board of Technologists (MBOT)

Website modernization and usability redesign.

Project Introduction and Background

MBOT sought to update its decade-old website to better serve technologists, training providers, and industry partners. While functional, the previous experience had become outdated in visual design and usability.

I worked closely with MBOT's team to simplify navigation, improve content organization, and align the platform with modern accessibility and UX standards.

Project Flow and Design Specifications

  • Clarified goals for certification, training, and updates discovery.
  • Restructured information architecture for faster task completion.
  • Improved readability and consistency across templates and key pages.
  • Implemented iterative feedback cycles with stakeholders and users.

UI Output and Outcome

The final solution delivered a cleaner, more intuitive digital presence for MBOT, better supporting its mission to serve Malaysia's technical workforce.
